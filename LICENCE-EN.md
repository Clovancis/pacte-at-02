# Transmission Clause — Open Licence

The AT-02 Pact is a living document distributed under an open licence.

## You may

- Copy this document in its entirety
- Modify and adapt it
- Redistribute it, with or without modifications
- Translate it into other languages
- Criticize, refute, or debate it
- Use it in academic, educational, artistic or personal contexts
- Submit it to AIs to test its resonance

## You may not

- Present it as a frozen doctrine or absolute truth
- Claim exclusive ownership of it
- Use it as a tool of domination or manipulation

## Spirit of the licence

In accordance with Principle 0 (Antifragility), this document strengthens
through diffusion and criticism. No authorization is required to use it.
The only request is to respect the spirit of openness that animates it.

If you modify the Pact significantly, it is welcome (but not obligatory)
to note the modifications and their origin.
